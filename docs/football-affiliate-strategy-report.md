# Football Affiliate Strategy Report

**Project:** WC26 Quiniela  
**Audience:** Spanish-speaking football fans globally, with emphasis on Mexico and US Hispanics  
**Research date:** June 2026  
**Goal:** Identify the best affiliate programs to pursue, exact registration links, earning potential, how many to include on-site, and compliance/implementation requirements.

## Executive Summary

The best launch strategy is **not** to chase every sportsbook affiliate program. Launch with **3-5 betting affiliate programs maximum**, split by geography: **Afiliago** for Mexico/LatAm sportsbook access, **Income Access** and/or **Vault Network** for US-regulated sportsbook/DFS access, and **Betway/Super Partners** or **bet365 Partners** as strong global/LatAm secondary options.[^1][^2][^3][^4] Add **2-3 non-betting affiliate layers** (Fanatics/Impact, Amazon Associates, Awin/Skimlinks) for jerseys, merch, travel, and experiences to reduce gambling-compliance risk and monetize traffic that cannot legally receive sportsbook CTAs.[^5][^6][^7][^8]

For Mexico, **Afiliago is the most efficient first application** because it is a Spanish-language iGaming affiliate network with public registration and confirmed LatAm-oriented brands such as Betsson, Codere, bwin, Sportium, Luckia, Rivalo, and YaJuego.[^1] For US Hispanics, use **geo-aware CTAs** and route sportsbook links only to legal states; otherwise show DFS, merch, travel, or email capture instead.[^9] Avoid **UpPromote** for this use case because it is a Shopify/e-commerce affiliate app, not a sportsbook affiliate network; avoid **BetAndDeal** because research could not verify a live, reputable affiliate operation.[^10][^11]

## Recommended Affiliate Mix

### Launch mix

Use **6-8 total monetization relationships**, but show only **2-4 contextual CTAs per page**:

| Slot | Purpose | Recommended program(s) | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| Primary Mexico sportsbook | Mexico/LatAm betting CTA | **Afiliago** first; pursue Codere/Betsson/bet365 directly after approval | Spanish/LatAm fit, multiple brands from one application |
| Primary US legal-state sportsbook | US Hispanic legal-state CTA | **Income Access** and/or **Vault Network** | Multi-brand US sportsbook access and regulated-market focus |
| Secondary global sportsbook | Backup / A-B testing | **Betway/Super Partners**, **bet365 Partners**, **Betsson Affiliates** | Strong football recognition; useful if accepted |
| Merchandise | Jerseys, kits, accessories | **Fanatics via Impact**, **Amazon Associates**, **Awin/Skimlinks** | Monetizes non-betting traffic; safer compliance profile |
| Travel/tickets | Host-city travel, tours, hotels | **Booking.com via CJ**, **Viator via Awin**, possibly Expedia/CJ | High-value WC 2026 travel intent |

### How many betting programs?

Start with **3 sportsbook relationships**:

1. **Afiliago** for Mexico/LatAm.
2. **Income Access or Vault Network** for US-regulated state traffic.
3. **Betway/Super Partners or bet365 Partners** as a recognizable football-focused secondary.

Do **not** place 10+ sportsbook logos on v1 pages. Too many operators dilute clicks, create compliance complexity, and make the site look like a thin affiliate doorway. The better pattern is one primary CTA, one comparison/alternative CTA, and one non-betting CTA per article.

## Mexico-Focused Sportsbook Programs

### 1. Afiliago — first application

Afiliago is the strongest first step for Mexico/LatAm because it is a Spanish-language iGaming affiliate network with a public registration page and multiple relevant LatAm brands under one roof.[^1] It is especially useful because some Mexico-relevant operators do not expose easy public affiliate links on their own websites.

**Apply:** `https://app.afiliago.com/registro`[^1]

**Why it fits:**

- Spanish-language onboarding and LatAm orientation.
- Confirmed partner-brand ecosystem includes Betsson, Codere, bwin, Sportium, Luckia, Rivalo, and YaJuego.[^1]
- One application can unlock multiple sportsbook relationships.
- Good fit for Spanish content and Mexico/LatAm traffic.

**Caveats:**

- Not US-regulated; do not use for US state-specific sports betting compliance.
- Commission details may be negotiated or revealed post-approval.

### 2. Codere — high Mexico relevance, hard direct path

Codere is highly relevant for Mexico, but the research did not verify a stable public direct affiliate signup path. The best practical route is to apply through Afiliago first because Codere was confirmed in Afiliago’s partner ecosystem.[^1]

**Recommended path:** Apply through Afiliago first.

**Why it matters:**

- Strong Spanish-language brand recognition.
- Mexico relevance is stronger than many offshore sportsbooks.

**Caveat:** If direct Codere affiliate contact becomes available, treat it as a high-priority direct relationship.

### 3. bet365 Partners

bet365 is a strong football brand and a valuable option for Mexico/LatAm audiences if accepted.[^12]

**Apply:** `https://www.bet365partners.com/`[^12]

**Caveats:**

- Some automated checks could not fetch the portal due to TLS/bot protection, but the domain is industry-known.
- Acceptance criteria and geography rules must be confirmed post-application.

### 4. Betway / Super Partners

Betway/Super Partners is a strong secondary option with an accessible registration URL.[^4]

**Apply:** `https://account.superpartners.com/?form=Registration`[^4]

**Why it fits:**

- Recognizable football/sportsbook brand.
- Useful as a backup/secondary CTA if Afiliago or bet365 applications take time.

### 5. Betsson Affiliates

Betsson is relevant for LatAm and appeared in Afiliago’s confirmed brand ecosystem.[^1][^13]

**Apply:** `https://www.betssonaffiliates.com/` or through Afiliago.[^1][^13]

**Recommended path:** Start via Afiliago; then apply direct if the program permits Mexico/LatAm traffic and offers better terms.

### Brands to be cautious with

Avoid leading with offshore/grey-market programs even if headline commissions are higher. For a YMYL betting-adjacent content site, trust, compliance, and long-term domain reputation matter more than a high CPA promise. Be cautious with brands that cannot clearly state licensed territories, responsible-gambling requirements, or accepted traffic geographies.

## US Hispanic / US-Regulated Sportsbook Programs

US sportsbook affiliate marketing is more restrictive than Mexico/LatAm because sports betting is state-regulated. A static global Spanish-language site should not show generic US sportsbook CTAs to all US visitors. Use geo-aware routing:

- Legal sports betting state: show approved sportsbook CTA for that state.
- Non-legal state: show DFS/picks, merch, travel, newsletter, or “where legal” educational content.
- Unknown geo: default to non-betting CTA or a comparison page with disclaimers.

### 1. Income Access Network

Income Access is a strong first application for US-facing affiliate access because it is a publisher-facing iGaming network and is owned by Paysafe.[^2]

**Apply:** `https://www.incomeaccessnetwork.com/registration.asp`[^2]

**Research-confirmed brand ecosystem:** FanDuel, Caesars, BetRivers, Hard Rock, William Hill, Golden Nugget, and other iGaming brands were identified in Income Access materials.[^2]

**Why it fits:**

- Focus on US regulated-state complexity.
- Potentially useful for both sportsbook and DFS/prediction-market offers.
- Good fallback for US Hispanics in states where sportsbook offers are unavailable.

### 3. FanDuel Affiliates

FanDuel is one of the strongest US sportsbook brands, but it is US-only and state-restricted.[^14]

**Apply:** `https://affiliates.fanduel.com/registration.asp`[^14]

**Implementation note:** Only show where legally available and approved by program terms.

### 4. BetMGM Partners

## Compliance Requirements for the Website

### Universal requirements

Every page with betting affiliate links should include:

- Clear affiliate disclosure near the link:  
  **ES:** “Podemos recibir una comisión si haces clic y te registras, sin costo adicional para ti.”  
  **EN:** “We may earn a commission if you click and sign up, at no extra cost to you.”
- Responsible-gambling disclaimer.
- Age warning: **18+ in Mexico / 21+ where required in the US**.
- “Where legal / donde sea legal” language.
- `rel="sponsored"` on affiliate links.

### Mexico traffic

For Mexico-facing pages:

- Prefer licensed/recognizable operators.
- Use Spanish copy and Mexican betting terminology such as “momios.”
- Avoid “guaranteed winner” claims.
- Do not imply the site is a gambling operator.
- Keep predictions as entertainment/information, not financial promises.

### US traffic

For US Hispanic traffic:

- Do not show US sportsbook CTAs without state-level eligibility logic.
- Suppress sportsbook offers in states where sports betting is illegal.
- Use DFS/picks/merch/travel alternatives where sportsbook offers are unavailable.
- Avoid paid social gambling ads unless the platform, operator, and jurisdiction approval requirements are satisfied.

### Social media

Organic content should frame posts as “pronóstico,” “análisis,” or “mi pick,” not direct gambling promotion. Do not put direct sportsbook links in TikTok/Instagram captions. Use site landing pages with disclosures and georouting instead.

## On-Site Implementation Plan

### 1. Affiliate link model

Create a simple data model/config file for affiliate offers:

```js
{
  id: 'afiliago-codere-mx',
  market: 'MX',
  category: 'sportsbook',
  label: 'Ver momios disponibles',
  url: process.env.CODERE_OR_AFILIAGO_URL,
  rel: 'sponsored nofollow',
  minAge: 18,
  disclosure: 'Podemos recibir una comisión si haces clic y te registras, sin costo adicional para ti.'
}
```

### 2. Geo-aware CTA logic

Priority:

1. Mexico visitor → Mexico/LatAm sportsbook offer (Afiliago/Codere/Betsson/bet365/Betway).
2. US legal-state visitor → approved US sportsbook offer (Income Access/Vault/FanDuel/BetMGM/etc.).
3. US non-legal/unknown → Fanatics/Amazon/Awin/Skimlinks merch or newsletter.
4. International unknown → generic “where legal” sportsbook comparison or merch.

### 3. Recommended page modules

| Page area | CTA |
|---|---|
| Top article intro | One soft CTA: “Consulta momios donde sea legal” |
| Odds section | Primary sportsbook CTA + disclosure |
| Footer/disclaimer block | Responsible gambling + affiliate disclosure |
| Sidebar/end of article | Jersey/merch CTA |
| Team pages | Team merch + travel/watch-party guides |

### 4. Keep launch simple

Launch with:

- 1 Mexico/LatAm primary sportsbook link.
- 1 US legal-state route.
- 1 global/secondary sportsbook.
- 1 merch partner.
- 1 travel/commerce partner.

Then measure clicks, signups, deposits, and rejection/approval quality before adding more.

## Risks and Open Questions

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| US state-by-state compliance | High | Use geo-aware CTAs; suppress sportsbook links where not legal |
| Mexico operator licensing uncertainty | High | Prefer clearly licensed/recognized operators and network-vetted offers |
| Thin affiliate perception | High | Limit number of sportsbook CTAs; prioritize original prediction content |
| Program rejection due to low traffic | Medium | Apply early; show live demo, content plan, compliance pages, traffic projections |
| Grey-market operator temptation | Medium/High | Avoid programs with unclear licensing despite high commissions |
| Commission terms hidden until approval | Medium | Track all offers in a spreadsheet after acceptance; compare CPA/revshare/EPC |
| Third-party directory quality | Medium | Use directories for discovery only; apply through official/operator/network pages |

## Confidence Assessment

**High confidence**

- Afiliago, Income Access, Vault Network, Betway/Super Partners, Fanatics/Impact, Amazon Associates, Awin, CJ, and Skimlinks are legitimate targets for this site’s affiliate strategy.
- UpPromote is not appropriate for sportsbook affiliate acquisition.
- BetAndDeal should be avoided unless the user can independently verify a current reputable operation.
- The site should not include dozens of sportsbook links at launch.

**Medium confidence**

- Exact commission rates for sportsbook programs, because many are private and revealed only after approval.
- Direct Codere affiliate availability; the safest current path is via Afiliago.
- Direct bet365/Betsson application behavior because portals may block automated checks.

**Low confidence / verify after approval**

- Which specific US sportsbook offers each network will approve for Spanish-language content.
- Which Mexico offers have the best real EPC for this audience.
- Whether each operator allows AI-assisted prediction content; disclose editorial process if asked.
